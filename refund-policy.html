Refund Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

Short version: Grove is free to download and try. If you buy a license and it's not for you, email hello@molayab.com within 14 days of purchase and we'll refund you in full — no questions asked.

1. Try before you buy

Grove can be downloaded and used as a trial before you pay for anything. We encourage you to install it, connect a real project, and confirm it fits your workflow before purchasing a license. Because a trial is available, we're able to offer a refund policy that's simple and generous rather than one full of exceptions.

2. 14-day money-back guarantee

If you purchase a Grove license and change your mind, contact us within 14 calendar days of the purchase date and we will issue a full refund to your original payment method — no reason required. This applies to every license tier (1, 3, and 5 Macs).

One refund per customer per license purchase. We reserve the right to decline repeat refund requests from the same customer or purchases that show a pattern of buy-refund abuse (for example, repeatedly purchasing and refunding to extend trial access, or requesting refunds across multiple accounts for the same person).

3. How to request a refund

Email hello@molayab.com with:

  • The email address used for the purchase
  • Your Gumroad order/receipt ID (found in your purchase confirmation email)
  • Optionally, why Grove didn't work out — this is genuinely useful feedback, but never required

We aim to respond within 2 business days and process approved refunds within 5–10 business days.

4. How refunds are processed

All purchases are handled by Gumroad, Inc., which acts as the merchant of record for transactions made through gumroad.com. Approved refunds are issued by Gumroad back to your original payment method, and are subject to Gumroad's own payment processing terms. Once a refund is issued, the associated license key is deactivated and can no longer be used to activate Grove.

5. After the 14-day window

We can't guarantee a refund for requests made after 14 days from purchase. That said, if you hit a significant bug that broke your workflow, or something else went genuinely wrong on our end, email us — we look at these on a case-by-case basis and would rather make it right than keep money we didn't earn.

6. Your statutory rights

Nothing in this policy limits any consumer protection rights you have under the law of your country or state that cannot be waived by agreement — for example, statutory withdrawal rights for digital content purchases in the European Union and United Kingdom, or consumer guarantees under Australian Consumer Law. Where local law grants you stronger rights than this policy, those rights apply. Where you have an EU/UK statutory right of withdrawal for digital content and you ask us to begin providing that content (e.g. by issuing your license key) before the withdrawal period ends, you acknowledge that this right may be lost once the content has been fully delivered, consistent with applicable law.

7. Chargebacks

If you're unhappy with a purchase, please email us before opening a chargeback or payment dispute with your bank or card issuer — we can almost always resolve it faster directly, and a chargeback outside our refund window will result in immediate deactivation of the associated license.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time, for example to reflect changes in how Gumroad processes payments. Changes apply to purchases made after the "Last updated" date above; a purchase you already made is governed by the policy in effect at the time you bought it.

9. Contact

Grove is built and supported by an independent developer, Mateo Olaya Bernal. For anything refund-related, reach out at hello@molayab.com.
